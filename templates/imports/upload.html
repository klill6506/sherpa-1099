Multi-Sheet Import

Your workbook can contain multiple form types. Each sheet will be imported separately:

  • Filer Information - Filer/payer details (required)
  • 1099-NEC - Nonemployee compensation
  • 1099-MISC - Miscellaneous income (rents, royalties, etc.)
  • 1099-S - Real estate transactions
  • 1098 - Mortgage interest

Column names will be auto-mapped to the following fields:

Recipient Info

  • name - Recipient name
  • tin or ssn - Tax ID
  • address - Street address
  • city, state, zip

1099-NEC

  • amount or compensation - Box 1
  • federal withheld - Box 4

1099-MISC

  • rents - Box 1
  • royalties - Box 2
  • other income - Box 3
  • medical - Box 6
